fragmenstein.branding submodule
===================================


Module contents
---------------

This contains two variables, ``feijoa``, a hexcode (``str``) for the Fragmenstein green,
and ``divergent_colors`` (``Dict[int, List[str]]``) are R-style CIE HChL divergent colours based on feijoa.
(see colour_note).
